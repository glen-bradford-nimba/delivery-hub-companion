Connect to your Salesforce org to manage work items and log hours.

Your Salesforce org URL (e.g., https://nimbasolutions.my.salesforce.com)

Get this from Salesforce CLI: sf org display --target-org MyOrg --json

This will remove your saved access token. Are you sure?

How to get your access token

  1. Open your terminal
  2. Run: sf org display --target-org YourOrg --json
  3. Copy the accessToken value from the JSON output
  4. Paste it above and click Save & Connect

Tokens expire periodically. You'll need to refresh when they do. The extension will notify you when your session expires.
